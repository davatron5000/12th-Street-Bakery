About

12th Street Bakery is a local social enterprise that strongly believes that personal development and education are critical in our society. The enterprise exists to serve as a training incubator to prepare students for living wage jobs as well as assist in developing life skills for a hope and future. We work diligently to ensure that the services and products we sell are used to treat and support the trainees, community members and their families fairly and with respect.
